Easy Cheese Biscuits

Total

Prep/Total Time: 30 min

Servings

1 dozen

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3⁄4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1⁄2 cup cold butter
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1⁄4 to 1⁄2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1-1⁄4 cups 2% milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450Â°. In a large bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, sugar, salt and cream of tartar. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in cheese, garlic and pepper flakes. Add milk; stir just until moistened.

  2. Drop dough by heaping 1⁄4 cupfuls 2 in. apart onto a greased baking sheet. Bake 18-20 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.

Nurtrition Facts

1 biscuit: 237 calories, 12g fat (7g saturated fat), 32mg cholesterol, 429mg sodium, 26g carbohydrate (2g sugars, 1g fiber), 7g protein.
